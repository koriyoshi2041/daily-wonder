Understanding is not a pile of notes. It is a route that has to keep working after the first answer looks plausible.

This sketch treats logic blocks as a switchboard. Drag a block to change the shape of the route; tap empty space to perturb the board; double tap a block to pin it; press **F** to freeze the current arrangement, **R** to reset, or **Space** to inject another signal.

The route integrity number is not a grade. It is a live reading of whether the blocks are near enough, balanced enough, and connected enough for an inference to keep moving.
