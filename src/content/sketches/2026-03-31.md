Self-replicating Forth agents as living organisms. Each unit starts with a few random words, gossips with nearby peers to share vocabulary, mutates its own code, and spawns children when fit enough. Underperformers get culled. The mesh breathes.

**Interactions:** Click to spawn new units. Press M to force-mutate all. Press E to evolve (cull weakest).

Inspired by: the spinthariscope (seeing single atoms act in darkness) and Unit's motto: "The language builds itself. The agent IS the language."
